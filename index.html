Dedicated and ambitious for passionate professional and personal growth, Pau Sabater Ncher is an undergraduate student of two simultaneous Bachelor Degrees in the fields of Mathematics and Physics Engineering at Universitat Politcnica de Catalunya under CFIS mentorizing. He was born in Algemes, a small town in Valencia, Spain, until he received a residence grant for continuing his studies in Barcelona. Driven by a desire to make a meaningful impact, he has recently invested his time learning Machine Learning and Hardware Design, after being intern at an industry-leading start-up in California and a very recognized Institute of Technology in the metropolitan area of Barcelona. He also relaxes by dancing fingers across both the violin's strings and the piano's keys, and he yearly travels with friends and family around the world to find out new cultures, experiences and perspectives.
